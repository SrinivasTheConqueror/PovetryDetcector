Sustainable Development Goal 1: No Poverty

End poverty in all its forms everywhere

Goal Why It Matters How We Can Help

The Goal

Sustainable Development Goal 1 aims to eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere by 2030, currently measured as people living on less than $1.90 a day. It also seeks to ensure that all people, especially the vulnerable, have access to basic resources, services, and opportunities to improve their lives.

Why It Matters

Poverty is a major barrier to sustainable development and human well-being. It limits access to education, healthcare, and opportunities, perpetuating cycles of inequality and hardship. Ending poverty is essential to building resilient societies and creating a world where everyone can thrive.

How We Can Help

  • Support local and global organizations working to reduce poverty.
  • Donate to initiatives that provide food, education, and healthcare to those in need.
  • Advocate for policies that promote social protection systems and equal access to opportunities.
  • Educate others about the importance of reducing poverty and encourage community action.
